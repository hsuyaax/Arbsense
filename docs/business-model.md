# ArbSense Business Model

## Target Users

1. DeFi traders seeking low-risk, delta-neutral arbitrage opportunities.
2. Market makers needing cross-platform pricing intelligence.
3. Prediction market platforms that benefit from tighter price efficiency.

## Revenue Model

1. Performance fee: charge 10% of net arbitrage profit generated by the agent.
2. Subscription: pro tier for real-time alerts, deeper analytics, and API access.
3. Future token path: ARBS governance and staking for priority strategy access.

## Ecosystem Value Proposition

ArbSense improves BNB Chain prediction market efficiency by identifying mispricings across fragmented platforms.
This helps traders capture opportunities while helping all users by reducing cross-platform price divergence.

## Roadmap

1. Months 1-2: BSC mainnet launch with 3 prediction-market integrations and alerting dashboard.
2. Months 3-4: opBNB-first high-frequency reporting and smart-wallet execution.
3. Months 5-6: Expand to additional EVM ecosystems with the same semantic matching core.
4. Months 7-12: ARBS governance/token utility, open API, and partner integrations.

## Go-To-Market

1. Launch with public demo dashboard and open-source repo.
2. Onboard early traders through Telegram/Discord alert channels.
3. Partner with prediction market communities for co-marketing and data integrations.
